Bitbrain: advanced neurotechnology

About the Company

Bitbrain is a company that specializes in the development of advanced neurotechnology solutions for research and industry. They focus on developing innovative reliable and user-friendly deviced for measuring electroencephalograms (EEG) signals from the brain. They also develop software for processing and analyzing these signals, as well as research within the neuroscience field.

My Experience

I worked there as an intern from September 2024 to February 2025, as part of my Final Bachelor Thesis. I was part of the Data Science team, where I worked on an innovative project that aimed to decode information about the cognitive state of elderly people from EEG signals measured during sleep. I attended also several Data Science project meetings, being able to learn how data science projects are carried out and how it is the research environment from the inside.

What I Learned

During my time at Bitbrain, I learned how to work with EEG signals, how to preprocess them and how to extract relevant information (features) from them. I also learned neurosience (especially, from sleep) concepts and how to sligthly apply them to the data science field. I also learned how to use AI models, both deep learning and classical machine learning, to classification tasks. I also learnt how to evaluate models, how to tune them and how to present the results to the team in the most accurate way possible.

My Mission

My mission was to try to decode information about the cognitive state of older adults from EEG signals measured during sleep. I had to extract features from the data (both sleep macrostructure and microstructure), train different models and evaluate them to see which one was the best for each task. Finally, I wrote a report with the worked done, and designed a presentation to show the results to both the company team and teh degree jury.
